Common Questions

Everything you need to know about SuqSphere

How is SuqSphere different from existing platforms?

SuqSphere uniquely combines social networking, job discovery, freelancing, and e-commerce into one unified experience designed specifically for Africa's digital economy. Most platforms focus on just one vertical—we bring them all together.

Is SuqSphere free to use?

Yes, creating a profile and connecting is completely free. We offer premium features and seller tools with flexible pricing options depending on your needs.

How does SuqSphere ensure safe transactions?

We use verified identities, escrow payments, user ratings, dispute resolution, and seller credibility tools to create a trusted environment for all transactions.

Can I use SuqSphere in my country?

SuqSphere is available across Africa with support for major local currencies and payment methods. Check our website for country-specific features.

How do I get paid for gigs or sales?

Payments are processed securely through our integrated payment system with local bank transfers, mobile money, and other local methods depending on your country.

What kind of support is available?

We offer 24/7 customer support through chat, email, and WhatsApp, plus comprehensive help documentation and creator resources.
